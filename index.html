Who We Are

I CUBE ENGINEERING LLP is an India-based engineering solutions company serving global industrial markets with high-quality mechanical component sourcing, gearbox spare parts, gearbox servicing and engineering design services.

We specialize in precision-engineered components such as gears, shafts, castings, machined parts and gearbox assemblies, supporting Helical, Bevel, Worm and Planetary gear systems across diverse industries worldwide.

Backed by strong engineering expertise and a qualified supplier network, we deliver cost-effective, quality-assured solutions that meet international standards. Our capabilities include reverse engineering, BOM development, technical consultancy and refurbishment of critical gearbox components, enabling reliable replacement and lifecycle support for overseas customers.

Guided by our core values—Innovation, Intelligence and Integrity—we focus on technical excellence, transparent communication and long-term partnerships. At I CUBE Engineering, we act as a dependable engineering partner committed to performance, precision and global reliability.

Comprehensive Mechanical & Engineering Solutions

Helical Gear Shaft

Our precision-engineered Helical Gear Shafts utilize angled teeth for smoother, quieter operation and high-load transmission. Ideal for heavy-duty automotive and industrial machinery where durability and efficiency are paramount.

Worm Gear Shaft

The Worm Gear Shaft offers high reduction ratios in a compact footprint, ensuring precise speed regulation. Perfect for elevators and conveyors, providing self-locking safety and smooth transmission.

Bevel Gears

Our Bevel Gears are engineered for high-efficiency power transmission in right-angle drives. Precision-cut for smooth engagement and reduced noise, they are ideal for heavy machinery, automotive differentials and industrial gearboxes requiring reliable torque transfer.
